TL;DR: The future of software dev is AI-augmented, integration-driven, domain-focused, and sustainability-conscious.

APIs are everywhere, whether it's SaaS, IoT, AI models, government data. The challenge is orchestration (stitching them together, handling data flow, resilience, security).

Instead of just "frontend/backend devs" I we'll see more domain specialists and product engineers. Devs who deeply understand the problem domain will stand out more than those who just know a framework.

Future devs will be part engineer, part systems thinker, part "tool orchestrator."

So, the best devs will be those who know how to ask the right questions, integrate the right tools, and validate the results.
